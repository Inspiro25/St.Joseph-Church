St.Joseph Church

St. Joseph's Church in Ulikkal, located in the Kannur district of Kerala, India, is a significant place of worship for the local Christian community. The church is part of the Diocese of Thalassery, which is dedicated to St. Joseph.

Ulikkal is a growing town situated approximately 7.5 km north of the taluk headquarters, Iritty, and about 46 km from the district headquarters, Kannur. The town is known for its harmonious community, where people of various religions and cultures coexist peacefully..
