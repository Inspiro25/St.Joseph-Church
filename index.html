Welcome to St. Joseph's Church

St. Joseph's Church in Arabi, Ulikkal, is a prominent Catholic parish located in the Kannur district of Kerala, India. The church is part of the Diocese of Thalassery, which is dedicated to St. Joseph.

The church is situated in the village of Arabi, within the Ulikkal Panchayat. Ulikkal is a hilly area known for its agrarian economy, with major crops including rubber, cashew, coconut, and areca nut. The region is also home to several other places of worship, such as St. Mary's Church in Thermala and St. George's Church in Puravayal.
